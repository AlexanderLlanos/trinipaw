OUR MISSION

We believe the journey of finding the right specialist and accurate treatment for your sick pet should be seamless and easy to understand. Our mission is to deliver the vital information, clarity, and community support that pet parents and their ‘fur babies’ need to thrive.

OUR APPROACH

We want to become the most trusted online resource for pet owners and leverage the power of collective knowledge and care to make quality health care universally accessible, manageable and equitable.
